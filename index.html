How to Play

Catch the falling stars with your basket!

Controls:

On iPhone/iPad: Tap directly where you want the basket to go

For Chrome on iPhone: Tap and hold, then drag left/right

On Desktop: Use arrow keys or move your mouse

Star Points:

Gold: 10 points
Blue: 15 points
Purple: 20 points
Red: 25 points
Green: 30 points

You have 3 lives
